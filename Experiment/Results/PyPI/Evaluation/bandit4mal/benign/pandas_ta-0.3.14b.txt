96	"""Schaff Trend Cycle (STC)
97	
98	The Schaff Trend Cycle is an evolution of the popular MACD incorportating two
99	cascaded stochastic calculations with additional smoothing.
100	
101	The STC returns also the beginning MACD result as well as the result after the
102	first stochastic including its smoothing. This implementation has been extended
103	for Pandas TA to also allow for separatly feeding any other two moving Averages
104	(as ma1 and ma2) or to skip this to feed an oscillator (osc), based on which the
105	Schaff Trend Cycle should be calculated.
106	
107	Feed external moving averages:
108	Internally calculation..
109	    stc = ta.stc(close=df["close"], tclen=stc_tclen, fast=ma1_interval, slow=ma2_interval, factor=stc_factor)
110	becomes..
111	    extMa1 = df.ta.zlma(close=df["close"], length=ma1_interval, append=True)
112	    extMa2 = df.ta.ema(close=df["close"], length=ma2_interval, append=True)
113	    stc = ta.stc(close=df["close"], tclen=stc_tclen, ma1=extMa1, ma2=extMa2, factor=stc_factor)
114	
115	The same goes for osc=, which allows the input of an externally calculated oscillator, overriding ma1 & ma2.
116	
117	
118	Sources:
119	    Implemented by rengel8 based on work found here:
120	    https://www.prorealcode.com/prorealtime-indicators/schaff-trend-cycle2/
121	
122	Calculation:
123	    STCmacd = Moving Average Convergance/Divergance or Oscillator
124	    STCstoch = Intermediate Stochastic of MACD/Osc.
125	    2nd Stochastic including filtering with results in the
126	    STC = Schaff Trend Cycle
127	
128	Args:
129	    close (pd.Series): Series of 'close's, used for indexing Series, mandatory
130	    tclen (int): SchaffTC Signal-Line length.  Default: 10 (adjust to the half of cycle)
131	    fast (int): The short period.   Default: 12
132	    slow (int): The long period.   Default: 26
133	    factor (float): smoothing factor for last stoch. calculation.   Default: 0.5
134	    offset (int): How many periods to offset the result.  Default: 0
135	
136	Kwargs:
137	    ma1: 1st moving average provided externally (mandatory in conjuction with ma2)
